About The Author

Hi! My name is Nguyen Dang Khoa Huynh, and I am a traveler. I love exploring cultures, landscapes, and meeting people from around the world. For this tourism website, I chose Ho Chi Minh City as my featured destination because of its unique blend of history, culture, and modernity. Formerly known as Saigon, this vibrant city is a melting pot of influences from its French colonial past, the Vietnam War, and its rapid development into a bustling urban hub.

I hope this website helps everyone learn more about this incredible city, from its rich history and iconic landmarks to its delicious street food and energetic atmosphere. I aim to inspire people to visit and experience the dynamic spirit of Ho Chi Minh City for themselves!
